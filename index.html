About Diana

Two years after my own wedding, I became enamored with the entire wedding process. It began by creating hand‑painted decor and invitations, evolving into the profound joy of uniting couples themselves.

I became licensed in 2024 to perform Christian, Christ‑centered ceremonies. I collaborate with couples to craft personalized vows grounded in Biblical principles, ensuring that each ceremony is a unique reflection of their love and faith.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling with my husband and our two Shih Tzus, Pancake and Waffles.
